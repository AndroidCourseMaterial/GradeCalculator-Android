Weighted Grade Calculator

What do I need to get on this test to get an "A" in the course?

Weighted Grade Calculator allows you to estimate your final grade in a particular class. Choose from point total or weighted percentages modes to match the way your professor or teacher grades.

Once you've set up the grade components for each course, use the built-in sliders to easily forecast your final grade for different scenarios.

Upgrade to full version through an in-app purchase to allow multiple courses as well as the ability to save courses between app launches.

How To Get It

  • Side-load the Signed Official Free APK (no purchase required to unlock full version)
  • Install from the Google Play product page (Google Play in-app purchase required to unlock full version)
